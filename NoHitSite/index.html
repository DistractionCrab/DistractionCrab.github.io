Who we are!

We are a group of challenge runners for the Soulsbornekiro games, who wanted to focus on community around these games. We focus primarily on No-Hit runs where do not get hit by any enemy while running through the games. We've pushed the limits of these games to the brink, and represent the pinacle of strategy and technique when it comes to this games.

How to Join

Anyone is welcome to participate in our community, the team is exclusive to those who have completed hitless runs. If you're interested in participating in other ways, you're welcome to contributed strategies, discussion, help with events, and just chat with us at our discord.
